Help Center

Welcome to the Dct24 Help Center. Find answers to frequently asked questions and get support for our platform.

How do I start an exam?

To start an exam, please log into your account, navigate to the desired course, and click the "Start Exam" button.

How can I see my results?

Your exam results are shown immediately after you submit the exam. You can also view past results from your user dashboard.

Is there a demo test available?

Yes, you can take a "Quick Demo Test" from the homepage without needing to log in.
